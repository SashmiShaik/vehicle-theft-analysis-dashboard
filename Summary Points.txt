                      SUMMARY POINTS ON VEHICLE THEFT ANALYSIS

->Auckland had the highest number of vehicle thefts, showing that theft incidents are more common in busy and highly populated areas.
->Trailers were the most stolen vehicle type, making them the primary target for vehicle thieves.
->Vehicle thefts did not remain constant throughout the year and showed noticeable increases during certain months.
->Standard vehicles were stolen more often than luxury vehicles, likely because they are more commonly available on the roads.
->Regions with higher population density generally experienced more vehicle thefts, suggesting that crowded urban areas are at greater risk.
